## 1. Scope of Services

The Provider agrees to deliver the following services to the Client:

1. **Website Redesign** — Complete redesign of the corporate website including UX research, visual design, and front-end development.
2. **CMS Integration** — Setup and configuration of a content management system with custom templates.
3. **Training & Handoff** — On-site training for the Client's content team (up to 8 hours).

<div class="note-important">

**Important:** Any work outside the scope defined above requires a written change order signed by both parties before work begins.

</div>

## 4. Intellectual Property

All deliverables become the property of the Client upon full payment. The Provider retains the right to showcase the work in its portfolio unless the Client opts out in writing.

## 5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary information shared during the engagement. This obligation survives termination of this agreement for a period of two (2) years.

## 6. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. In case of termination:

- The Client pays for all work completed to date
- The Provider delivers all work-in-progress materials
- Unused prepayments are refunded within 15 business days

## 7. Limitation of Liability

The Provider's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Oregon, United States.
