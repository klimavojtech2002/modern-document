# Website Redesign Proposal

ACME Corporation — Digital Transformation Initiative

</div>

**Client:** ACME Corporation
**Date:** March 15, 2026
**Prepared by:** Jane Mitchell, Senior Consultant — Apex Digital

---

## Executive Summary

ACME Corporation's current website no longer reflects the quality of its products or the expectations of its customers. We propose a full redesign focused on performance, accessibility, and conversion optimization. This document outlines our approach, timeline, and budget.

## Project Phases

<div class="steps">
<div class="step"><div class="step-num">1</div><div class="step-content">

### Discovery & Audit

Stakeholder interviews, analytics review, and competitive benchmarking. Deliverables include a UX audit report and revised site architecture.

</div></div>
<div class="step"><div class="step-num">2</div><div class="step-content">

### Design & Prototyping

High-fidelity mockups for all key pages, interactive prototype for user testing, and a finalized design system with reusable components.

</div></div>
<div class="step"><div class="step-num">3</div><div class="step-content">

### Development & Launch

Front-end and back-end implementation, CMS integration, QA testing across devices, and staged rollout with post-launch monitoring.

</div></div>
</div>
